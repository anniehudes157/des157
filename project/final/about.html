About

An Elder’s Day is an interactive and immersive visual novel that serves to provide understanding on the physical and mental health of elders. The story utilizes a first-person perspective to provide an narrative that describes the loneliness that many elders feel.

Loneliness is of the major risk factors of mental health problems in elders. By 2050, 180 million to 400 million elders will be suffering from mental health issues.

So why isn’t this something that we think about?

Solving this problem isn't as hard as you think.

These simple actions can help reduce the loneliness of the elders you love:

  • Spend time with them.
  • Hold sincere conversations with them.
  • Express to them that they're loved.

For more information on the mental health of elders, visit:

  • World Health Organization
  • National Council on Aging

You can also support by donating to foundations related to elders mental health:

  • Geriatric Mental Health Foundation
