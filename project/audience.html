Background Research

Brad Cooper

General Profile

  • Occupation: Data Analyst
  • Age: 45
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: San Francisco, CA
  • Education: B.S. degree in Computer Science
  • Family: Married with 2 kids, living with mother

Goals

  • As a father of two children and head of the household, Brad takes on the responsibility of supporting the whole family financially. With the increased cost of living and the wish of giving his children the best education possible, he feels the constant pressure of having to climb up the corporate ladder to fufill his family's needs.

Challenges

  • The result of wanting to give his family the best has taken a toll on Brad. He works everyday and has little to no time to rest. As a result, he returns home mentally and physically tired. As a result, he rarely has time to spend with his family members.

Values

  • Brad truly values his family. In fact, their comfort of living is his first priority. At the same time, he values good education, and believes that it is the true path to success. He views filial piety fairly high, as he continues to support his mother at his home.

Fears

  • With the constant economic changes, Brad is often worried about his job stability and ability to support his family. He is afraid of disappointing his family members. Brad is also worried about the future of his mom, who is growing older by the day and has several health problems.
