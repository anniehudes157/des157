Audience Test

Thank you for volunteering to be part of my audience test! Your feedback is greatly appreciated. My project is still a work in progress, so many things are still under devlopement. Therefore, before you begin, please be aware of the following:

  • Try not to use the refresh button, as it will erase the information you've submitted.
  • The story is not fully completed, so the plot may not make much sense.
  • The form is still under construction, so only the name can be personalized.

Now, you're ready to do the perform the test! Please do the following tasks and answer the questions provided.

Tasks

  1. Navigate at your own pace and try to get to the end of the story.
  2. You want to go back to the beginning of the story again. How would you go about doing that?
  3. Navigate through the story again, but move around to pages that you are more interested in.
  4. You submitted the form, but want to change the information on it while reading the story. How will you do that?

Thank you for finishing the tasks! You are awesome! Now, please answer the following questions (bullet points are okay!):

Questions

  1. What do you think this website is about? What do you think is its purpose?
  2. What did you like about the website?
  3. What did you find confusing, boring or frustrating?
  4. Would you prefer the current art style or something more clean and cartoon, like this?
  5. What other elements do you think can be added to the website?
  6. Were you aware that you were an elder in the story? When did you realize it?
  7. Were you able to navigate through the website smoothly? Why or why not?
  8. Last but not least, did you learn or realize anything through the experience?

Again, thank you so much for doing the test! Your feedback is very helpful for the project. I hope you'll have a nice day!
